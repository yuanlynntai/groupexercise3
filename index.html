Exercise 3.

Virus

Thoughts

For this exercise, we decided to show how fast a virus can spread through our work. By moving the mouse, 1 ball will be created. As the mouse moves faster, the amount of balls created will increase even faster. This shows how fast a virus could spread, just like how humans are capable of spreading virus without knowing.

Issues

Through our group feedback we realised that there was no indication for the theme of "Virus". There was no mouse hover graphic to represent the theme chosen as it was just dots.

Achievements

We took away the importance of user interaction and concept introduction for the sketch, as it was crucial in playing the "understanding" of our sketch to users.

Testing

After applying the "Virus" graphic onto the mouse to show a strong message of the spreading of virus for our sketch. We conducted a user testing with another group of students.

Feedback

User testings indicated that the beige background did not show a strong contrast for the moving dots and its colours. The beige background did not allow a strong contrast.
